Valen vs Strong

Strong gets out of your way. Valen tells you the way. Two opposite ideas of what an app should do.

Written to be fair. Pricing verified July 2026.

The short version

Strong is the fastest, cleanest manual logger there is. It has no AI, no social feed and no opinions — you decide the workout, it records it beautifully, and it stays out of your way. If your programme already lives in your head or your coach's spreadsheet, nothing logs it faster.

Valen is for the opposite person: someone who doesn't want to decide the workout. It plans the session, coaches you through it, and adjusts as you go — and it keeps your data on your phone. If "just log what I already planned" describes you, Strong wins. If "tell me what to do" describes you, that's Valen.

Where Strong is genuinely ahead

Nothing logs a set faster. Strong has spent years making the core logging flow frictionless, and it shows — auto-filled previous weights, the cleanest interface in the category, and the best Apple Watch app going, so you can run a whole session from your wrist. If you lift on iOS with a watch and just want to record what you did, it's superb.

It's also stable and proven, with a one-time lifetime price. If you want a tool that does exactly the same reliable job in five years, that's a real virtue — and one a young app like ours can't claim yet.

Where Valen is different

It decides, so you don't have to. This is the whole split. Strong is a blank canvas that waits for you to bring the plan. Valen brings the plan — it builds the session, coaches you through it, and adjusts your targets from what you logged. If deciding the workout is the part that puts you off going, that's exactly the gap Valen was built to close.

Your data stays on your device. Like most trackers, Strong syncs to the cloud. Valen keeps your history local — we can't see it.

There's a coach in it. Strong is deliberately silent. Valen has a voice — one honest character that reflects on your training and won't flatter a bad week. Whether you want that or find it noise is genuinely down to you.

Choose Strong if…

You already know your programme, you want the fastest possible logging and the best Apple Watch app, you value a stable tool that won't change, and you'd rather the app stayed silent. It's the best pure logger there is.

Choose Valen if…

You don't want to plan the session yourself, you want a coach that decides and adjusts, and you want your data to stay on your phone. Strong records your decisions; Valen makes them with you.
