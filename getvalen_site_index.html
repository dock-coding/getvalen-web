Most fitness apps hand you a blank screen and a library of 800 exercises. Then they wait for you to figure it out.

Valen was built the other way round. You show up — it already knows the plan.

What it does

A coach, not a spreadsheet

Valen decides what you train, adjusts as you go, and tells you the truth about how it's going.

It plans the session

Open the app and today's workout is already there — built around your goal, your history and what you trained last. No blank page, no deciding.

It reads your numbers

Every set you log feeds back in. Valen tracks your lifts, spots your personal bests, and adjusts your next session's targets to match how you're actually moving.

It won't flatter you

No hollow praise. If a week slipped, Valen says so — kindly — and gives you the next step. When you've genuinely earned it, you'll know it meant something.

Track without an account

Log workouts, hit PBs, earn milestones — all with no sign-up. You only ever create an account if you want the AI coach.

Milestones worth sharing

PBs, streaks and milestones become clean cards you can post — proof of the work, not a leaderboard you have to perform on.

Yours, and only yours

Your workouts, body weight and history live on your device. We can't see them. Nobody can. See below for exactly what that means.

Privacy by architecture

Your data never leaves your phone

Every other strength app keeps your entire training history on its servers. Valen doesn't have servers for your data. Your workouts, programmes, personal bests and body weight are stored only on your device.

The only things that ever leave are your email — if you make an account — and your message to the coach, with a training summary, when you ask it something. We designed it this way on purpose, and we can't undo it even if we wanted to.
