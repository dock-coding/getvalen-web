02 What this policy covers

This policy covers the Valen mobile app and the services it connects to. It explains what personal data we handle, why, where it goes, how long it stays, and what you can do about it.

03 What stays on your device

The following is stored locally on your phone only. It is never uploaded to us or to anyone else. We have no copy of it, and we cannot access it:

  • Your name and training goal
  • Your height, body weight and body-weight history, date of birth, and biological sex
  • Your training location and available equipment
  • All workouts, sets, reps and weights
  • Programmes you build or follow
  • Personal bests, strength scores, badges and milestones
  • Water intake
  • App settings and theme preferences

A consequence worth understanding. Because we hold no copy, we cannot restore this data for you. If you uninstall Valen, change phone, or clear the app's data, your training history is gone. We do not yet offer a backup or transfer feature. We are working on one — but until it exists, this is the trade-off that comes with data that never leaves your phone.

4.3   AI coaching — this is the important one

When you send a message to Valen Coach, we pass your message, together with relevant context from your training data, to Anthropic — the company that provides the AI model behind the coach.

That context can include:

  • Recent workouts, exercises, sets, reps and weights
  • Personal bests and strength scores
  • Your training goal, experience level and available equipment
  • Your body metrics — body weight, height, age and biological sex
  • Your name, if you have given one

Anthropic processes this to generate the coach's reply and returns it to your phone. Neither we nor Anthropic use your data to train AI models. We do not store the conversation — once the reply reaches your phone, it lives on your device like everything else in section 3.

ProcessedAnthropic, United States — see section 6
Lawful basisContract — Article 6(1)(b) — and your explicit consent for the health-related elements — Article 9(2)(a). See section 5

If you never use the coach, none of this happens. Nothing in this section applies to you unless you sign in and send a message.

4.4   Feedback

If you send feedback through the app, what you type is delivered to us via Formspree, a form-handling service based in the United States. Please don't include anything sensitive in a feedback message. Whatever you write, we will read.

Lawful basis: our legitimate interest in improving the app — Article 6(1)(f).

05 Health data and your consent

Some of what Valen handles — your body weight, date of birth, biological sex and training history — counts as health data under UK and EU data protection law, which gives it stronger protection than ordinary personal data.

While that information stays on your phone, we are not processing it at all, and no special rules are engaged.

The moment it matters is when you use the coach, because that is when health-related context is transmitted to Anthropic. For that we rely on your explicit consent (Article 9(2)(a) UK GDPR). You give that consent when you sign in to use the coach, and we tell you plainly at that point what will be sent and to whom.

You can withdraw it at any time — by not using the coach, by signing out, or by deleting your account. Withdrawing consent does not affect anything that has already happened, and it does not touch your training data. Your workouts stay on your device either way.

07 What we do not do

These are choices, not accidents:

  • No analytics. Valen contains no analytics SDK. We do not know how many workouts you log, which screens you visit, or how often you open the app.
  • No crash reporting. We collect no crash or telemetry data.
  • No advertising. No ad networks, no ad identifiers, no ad tracking.
  • No third-party tracking of any kind. There are no tracking SDKs in the app.
  • No selling or sharing your data. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it for advertising or marketing. We never will.
  • No storage of your coach conversations. We keep counts, not content.
  • No AI training on your data. Neither we nor Anthropic use what you send to train models.

09 Deleting your account and your data

You have two separate controls and they do different things. That distinction is deliberate.

Delete your account

In the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

This permanently deletes your account, your email address, and your usage counts from our systems. It cannot be undone. You will be signed out and Valen Coach will be locked.

Your workouts stay on your device. Deleting your account does not touch your training history. If you no longer want the AI coach, you can delete your account and carry on using Valen exactly as before, with everything you have logged intact.

Erase your local data

In the app: Profile → Settings → Erase All Data on This Device.

This wipes everything in section 3 — workouts, programmes, personal bests, profile, the lot. It is permanent, and we cannot recover it, because we never had it. Uninstalling the app has the same effect.

If you have already uninstalled Valen

You can still have your account deleted. Email privacy@getvalen.app from the address you signed up with, asking us to delete your account. We will delete it and confirm, normally within 30 days.

10 Your rights

Under UK and EU data protection law you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, correct it if it is wrong, delete it (section 9), restrict or object to how we use it, receive it in a machine-readable format (portability), and withdraw consent where we rely on it (section 5).

In practice, the data we hold about you is small: your email address, an account identifier, sign-in timestamps, and monthly usage counts. Everything else is on your phone, where it is already yours.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@getvalen.app. We will respond within one month.

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data, you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office, the UK's data protection regulator.

12 Security

Sign-in uses a one-time code sent to your email, so there is no password to steal. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit. Coaching requests are authenticated, so an account can only ever see its own usage.

We keep as little as possible, which is itself a security measure: data we do not hold cannot be breached.

No system is perfectly secure, and we will not pretend otherwise. If a breach ever affects your personal data, we will notify you and the ICO as the law requires.
